TERMS AND CONDITIONS
This mortgage is to secure the payment of the principal sum of TWO HUNDRED THOUSAND

interest combined of ONE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS ($1,100.00), beginning on the 
1st day of March, 2024, and continuing on the same day of each succeeding month 
thereafter until the 1st day of March, 2034, when the remaining unpaid balance of 
principal and interest shall be due and payable.
